Our Mission

There is undoubtedly a difference between education and training. Through the professional experience of the academy's team over decades, we have realized the importance of training in refining and developing the skills of youth to join the labor market efficiently and effectively. Completing educational stages provides the foundational knowledge to begin practicing any profession. Here comes the role of training — to place young people on the first steps of practical application and professional practice. Moreover, training is not limited to recent graduates. Given the rapid and balanced advancements in all fields, there is a constant need for ongoing training, development, and human resource enhancement, which is the true wealth of our beloved country.

the state spares no effort in supporting and developing its real wealth — its youth — and encouraging them to work and produce, we felt the responsibility to take part in this mission effectively. Thus, we established the Professional Excellence Academy for Training, which is planned to offer a range of training programs that balance the state's objectives of supporting and encouraging citizens to work in all sectors, and the needs of companies looking for qualified and skilled individuals who possess the capabilities to achieve their goals.
